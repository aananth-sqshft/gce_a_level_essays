Topic Sentences

Build them up into topic sentences:

  • a. Providing free healthcare for all citizens is desirable in principle as it equalises access and ensures all citizens are given the opportunity to lead full, fulfilling lives.
  • b. Providing free healthcare for all citizens is desirable in practice as it pays off when citizens reciprocate by being more efficient at work and engaged in civic affairs.
  • c. Providing free healthcare for all citizens is not desirable as it may be less fair, not more, that the richest benefit comparatively little (because they go to private doctors) while the poorest benefit comparatively much (because they pay the least taxes).
  • d. Providing free healthcare for all citizens is not desirable as it may inadvertently incentive irresponsible behaviour, leaving citizens less healthy when they neglect to manage their own food intakes or fitness regimes.
